Springtime in Swarthmore

Spring Foliage in Swarthmore, Pennsylvania

These photos were taken on April 10th, 2021 in the borough of Swarthmore in Delaware County, Pennsylvania. Swarthmore is a small town in southeast Pennsylvania, very close to Philadelphia (20 miles). Swarthmore is famous for being home to Swarthmore College, a liberal arts college founded in 1864.
