New York City

Motivation

As a North Jersey native, I have made the trip to The City many times over the years. After all my visits, I have come to believe that New York is the best city in the United States, and perhaps all of North America. The five boroughs are rich in history and they all have their own unique charm. Here are some photos I have been gathering of New York, NY from 2018-2021.
